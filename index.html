Physics Department

         Physics Department at Walchand College of Engineering Established in 1947, it has Two faculty members and Three lab assistants.


        We have a Ph.D. programme in various areas of experimental and theoretical physics and two masters programmes in Optical Engineering and Solid State Technology. In addition, the department offers several courses that constitute the core of the B.Tech. Engineering Physics programme.

Our Profiles

Research in the department focuses on the following areas:

  • Quantum Information
  • Atomic and Molecular Physics
  • Condensed Matter Physics
  • Nanotechnology
  • Nonlinear Dynamics
  • Statistical Physics
